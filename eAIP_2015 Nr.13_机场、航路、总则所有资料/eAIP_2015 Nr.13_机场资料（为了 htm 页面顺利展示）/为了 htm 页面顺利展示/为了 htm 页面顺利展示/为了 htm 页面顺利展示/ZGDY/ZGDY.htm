ZGDY AD 2.22 飞行程序 Flight procedures

除经塔台特殊许可外,在张家界塔台管制区内的飞行,必须按照仪表飞行规则进行。

Flights within Zhangjiajie Tower Control Area shall operate under IFR unless special clearance has been obtained from Tower Control.

起落航线只准在跑道北侧进行,起落航线高度:850-900米。

Traffic circuits shall be made to the north of RWY, at the altitude of 850-900m.
