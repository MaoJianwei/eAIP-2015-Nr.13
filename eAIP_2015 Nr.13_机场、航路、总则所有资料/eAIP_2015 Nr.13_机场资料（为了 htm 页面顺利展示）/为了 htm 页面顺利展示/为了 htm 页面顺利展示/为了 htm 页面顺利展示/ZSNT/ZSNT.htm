1.2 机场运行以PBN程序为主用程序。所有进港航班到场前30分钟联系塔台,报告PBN运行能力。

1.2 PBN flight procedures are primary; Arrival aircraft shall inform the PBN capability to ATC 30mins in advance.

2. 起落航线 Traffic circuits

起落航线通常在跑道西侧进行,经ATC允许也可在东侧进行;起落航线高度:A、B类航空器高度450米, C、D类航空器高度450米-600米。

The traffic circuits shall be regularly in the west of airdrome or in the east of airdrome with ATC clearance at the altitude of 450m for aircraft CAT A/B and 450-600m for aircraft CAT C/D.

3. 仪表飞行程序 IFR flight procedures

3.1 严格按照航图中公布的进、离场程序飞行。如果需要,航空器可在空中交通管制部门指定的航路、导航台或定位点上空等待或做机动飞行;

3.1 Strict adherence is required to the relevant arrival/departure procedures published in the aeronautical charts. Aircraft may, if necessary, hold or maneuver on an airway, over a navigation facility or a fix designated by ATC;

3.2 实施优先着陆的飞机,经管制员允许后,按规定的飞行程序进近降落。情况紧急时,在确保飞行安全的前提下,经ATC允许可直接降落。

3.2 Aircraft shall take prior landing after ATC clearance and follow the ATC instructions. In emergencies, aircraft can landing with ATC clearance in a condition that ensured the flight safety.

ZSNT AD 2.23 其他资料 Other information

机场全年有鸟类活动。主要鸟击高危种类有:稚鸡、喜鹊、家燕、树麻雀、白鹭、池鹭、夜鹭等。机场已积极采取多种驱鸟方式,以降低鸟害。在跑道两侧75米以外装有鸟网。

Activities of bird flocks are found all the year round. Main type of bird are pheasant, magpie, swallow, sparrow, aigrette. Aerodrome Authority resorts to dispersal methods to reduce bird activities. Birds nets were installed at 75m outside both sides of RWY.
