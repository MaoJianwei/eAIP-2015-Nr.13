2.3 机场冲突多发地带运行要求

2.3 Hot spot procedure

2.3.1 机动区冲突多发地带位置见ZHHH AD2.24-1,2;

2.3.1 Refer to ZHHH AD2.24-1,2;

2.3.2 为减少运行差错,降低地面冲突和跑道入侵事件的发生概率,在机场活动区内运行的航空器需严格按照下述的要求运行:

2.3.2 For the purpose of reducing errors that lead to ground conflicts and runway incursions, aircraft operating within the maneuvering area must follow the requirements below:

HS1:B2、C6及B滑行道交叉区域 

禁止ACN值大于45的航空器从B2滑行道进出跑道;

当使用22号跑道时,在B滑行道上向东北方向滑行的航空器,如观察到有航空器正从B2滑行道脱离跑道时,应主动在B2、C6滑行道以外等待,避让脱离跑道的航空器。

HS1: intersections of taxiways B2、C6 and B

PCN of taxiway B2 is merely 45, the aircraft ACN exceeding 45 is prohibited to enter or vacate runway via taxiway B2;

When runway 22 is in use, traffic taxiing northeast on taxiway B should hold short of taxiway B2 or C6, to avoid traffic vacating runway via taxiway B2.   

HS2:C5及B滑行道交叉区域 

当使用04号跑道时,在B滑行道上向西南方向滑行并准备在C5道口进停机坪的航空器,如果观察到C5道口有航空器滑出时,应主动在C5滑行道以外等待,避让C5道口滑出的航空器;

当使用22号跑道时,在B滑行道上向东北方向滑行的航空器,如果观察到B滑行道上有相对滑行的航空器时,应在C5滑行道以外等待,如该航空器已越过C5滑行道,应立即原地等待,同时向管制员报告。

HS2: intersections of taxiways C5 and B

When runway 04 is in use, traffic on taxiway B which intends to enter apron at taxiway C5 should hold short of taxiway C5, to avoid traffic exiting apron via taxiway C5;

When runway 22 is in use, in case of head to head traffic observed on taxiway B, traffic taxiing northeast on taxiway B should hold short of taxiway C5, if this traffic has proceeded beyond taxiway C5, it must hold position and inform ATC immediately, in order to avoid opposite traffic on taxiway B.

HS3:B1、C4及B滑行道交叉区域

当使用22号跑道时,在B滑行道上向东北方向滑出的航空器,如观察到B1滑行道上有航空器脱离跑道时,应主动在C3滑行道以外等待,避让脱离跑道的航空器。

HS3: intersections of taxiways B1、C4 and B

When runway 22 is in use, traffic taxiing northeast on taxiway B should hold short of taxiway C3, to avoid traffic vacating runway via taxiway B1.

HS4:C3及B滑行道交叉区域

当使用04号跑道时,在B滑行道上向西南方向滑行的航空器,如观察到C3滑行道有滑出停机坪的航空器时,应主动在C4滑行道以外等待,避让C3道口滑出的航空器;

当使用22号跑道时,从C3滑出加入B滑行道的航空器,如果观察到B滑行道上航空器有冲突时,应主动在B滑行道以外等待,避让B滑行道的航空器。

HS4: intersections of taxiways C3 and B

When runway 04 is in use, traffic taxiing southwest on taxiway B should hold short of taxiway C4, to avoid traffic exiting apron via taxiway C3;

When runway 22 is in use, traffic exiting apron via taxiway C3 should hold short of taxiway B, to avoid traffic taxiing on taxiway B.

HS5:C1、C2及B滑行道交叉区域

当使用04号跑道时,在B滑行道上向西南方向滑行的航空器,如观察到C1、C2滑行道有滑出停机坪的航空器时,应主动在C2滑行道以外等待,避让C1、C2道口滑出的航空器。

HS5: intersections of taxiways C1、C2 and B

When runway 04 is in use, traffic taxiing southwest on taxiway B should hold short of taxiway C2, to avoid traffic exiting apron via taxiway C1 or C2.

ZHHH 2.21 噪音限制规定及减噪程序 Noise restrictions regulations and noise abatement procedures

1. 起飞减噪程序

1. Noise abatement procedures for departure

    在保证安全超障和飞行程序最低爬升梯度的条件下,执行如下起飞减噪程序。由于非管制原因不执行减噪程序,飞行员必须在起飞前告知管制员并说明原因(特殊飞行除外)。

In condition of complying with the requirements of obstacle clearance and climb gradient required by flight procedure, the following noise abatement climb procedures shall be implemented. If the procedures can not be implemented due to any reason except ATC, pilot shall inform the controller with a reasonable explanation(except for special flight).

1.1 在航空器起飞性能运行允许的情况下,尽可能使用减推力起飞;

1.1 The derated take-off is strongly recommended if the take-off performance of aircraft permit;

1.2 在高度450米时, 起始爬升速度V2+20km/h(10海里/小时),减小功率和俯仰角,保持可靠襟翼和速度继续爬升;

1.2 At altitude 450m, with a climb speed of V2 plus 20km/h(10kt), reduce engine power/thrust and angle of pitch, maintain a speed with flaps and slats in the take-off configuration;

1.3 高度900米以上时,平稳加速至航路爬升速度,按规定收襟翼/缝翼。

1.3 At altitude 900m or above, maintain a positive rate of climb, accelerate smoothly to en-route climb speed and retract flaps/slats on schedule.

ZHHH AD 2.22 飞行程序 Flight procedures
1. 总则 General

除经塔台特殊许可外,在塔台管制区内的飞行,均需按照仪表飞行规则进行。

Flights within Tower Control Area shall operate under IFR unless special clearance has been obtained from Tower Control.

2. 起落航线 Traffic circuits

起落航线在跑道西北侧,高度400-700米。

Traffic circuits shall be made to the northwest of RWY, at the altitudes of 400m-700m.

3. 仪表飞行程序 IFR flight procedures

严格按照航图中公布的进、离场程序飞行。如果需要,航空器可在空中交通管制部门指定的航路、导航台或定位点上空等待或做机动飞行。

Strict adherence is required to the relevant arrival/departure procedures published in the aeronautical charts. Aircraft may, if necessary, hold or maneuver on an airway, over a navigation facility or a fix designated by ATC.

4. 雷达程序和/或ADS-B程序 Radar procedures and/or ADS-B procedures

4.1武汉进近管制区域内实施雷达管制。航空器最小水平间隔为6千米;

4.1 Radar control within Wuhan APP has been implemented. The minimum horizontal radar separation is 6km;

4.2 雷达引导与排序

4.2 Radar vectoring and sequencing

a. 通常,航空器从河口VOR(HOK)、天门VOR(WTM)、龙口VOR(LKO)、浠水VOR(XSH)得到进近雷达引导和排序,直至相应的最后进近航迹或目视跑道。根据航空器性能或管制规定,发布雷达引导、上升或下降高度及速度调整的指令,使航空器之间保持规定的雷达间隔或尾流间隔;

a. Normally, aircraft will be vectored and sequenced from Hekou VOR(HOK), Tianmen VOR(WTM), Longkou VOR(LKO) and Xishui VOR(XSH) to the appropriate final approach track or to the time when RWY is in sight. Instructions about radar vectors, ascent/descent altitudes or speed adjustment will be issued for spacing and separating the aircraft so that stipulated radar intervals and wake intervals are maintained, taking into account aircraft characteristics or control regulations;

b. 离场航空器在起飞前收到ATC放行或塔台管制员给出起飞限制条件,起飞后可由管制员雷达引导离场。

b. If the departure aircraft receive take-off limits from controller, then it will be vectored to join in the standard departure routes by radar controller.
