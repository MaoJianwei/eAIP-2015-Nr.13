ZSTX AD 2.22 飞行程序 Flight procedures
1. 总则 General

除经塔台特殊许可外,在塔台管制区内的飞行,必须按照仪表飞行规则进行。

Flights within Tower Control Area shall operate under IFR unless special clearance has been obtained from Tower Control.

2. 起落航线 Traffic circuits

起落航线均在跑道西南侧进行,起落航线高度(QNH):A、B类为550米, C类为650米。

Traffic circuits shall be made to the southwest of RWY, at the altitude of 550m for aircraft CAT A/B, and 650m for aircraft CAT C/D.

3.1严格按照航图中公布的进、离场程序飞行。如果需要,航空器可在ATC指定的航路、导航台或定位点上空等待或做机动飞行。

3.1 Strict adherence is required to the relevant arrival and departure procedures published in the aeronautical charts. Aircraft may, if necessary, hold or maneuver on an airway, over a navigation facility or a fix designated by ATC.

3.2优先着陆:实施优先着陆的航空器,经ATC许可,按ATC指令和规定的进近程序实施优先着陆。

3.2 Priority landing: With the prior permission of ATC, the aircraft shall make the priority landing according to the instructions of ATC and the designated approach procedures.

5. 无线电通信失效程序 Radio communication failure procedures

航空器在确定机载无线电通信设备失效后:

When an airborne communication equipment failure is confirmed:

5.1 利用一切可利用的通信手段,尽快与机场塔台建立联系;

5.1 Aircraft shall contact ATC control with any available communication methods;

5.2 已飞越起始进近定位点的航空器,按标准进近程序进近着陆;

5.2 Aircraft which has already flown over IAF continue landing according to the standard IAP;

5.3 未飞越起始进近定位点的航空器,按照管制员给定的最后一个指令高度沿进场航线飞向TXN台,过台后加入标准仪表等待程序,在等待航线上保持高度做15分钟的等待后方可下降至修正海压高度1500米,再次过台后按仪表进近程序进近着陆;

5.3 Aircraft which has not flown over IAF fly to TXN at the latest height which is given from ATC according to the arrival procedure; join the holding procedure and hold 15 minutes following the standard holding procedure, descend to 1500m(QNH) and continue landing according to the standard IAP;

5.4 航空器在最后进近阶段通过DA或MDA前,必须取得目视参考方可着陆。

5.4 Aircraft shall obtain visual reference for landing prior to DA or MDA in the final approach.

6. 目视飞行程序 Procedures for VFR flights

6.1 等待:在机场上空,按起落航线进行等待。

6.1 Holding: aircraft could hold following the traffic circuits mentioned above.

6.2 进、离场规定

6.2 Arrival and departure regulations

进场:经ATC许可,符合目视气象条件进场的航空器,可按照目视飞行规则飞行,航空器驾驶员对航空器与障碍物的间隔负责。

Arrival: If VMC is fulfilled, with the prior permission of ATC, aircraft may fly according to VFR. Pilots are responsible for the separation between aircraft and obstacles.

离场:经ATC许可,沿起落航线爬升至1000米以上离场,入航后继续爬升至规定的航线高度。

Departure: With the prior permission of ATC, aircraft shall climb along the traffic circuits to 1000m or above and then join in en-ruote flight and keep climbing to the designated altitude.
