所有飞行需事先申请,并得到空中交通管制部门批准后方可进行。

180°turnaround on TWY is forbidden for all aircraft; Turn and 180°turnaround on the middle part of RWY are forbidden for MD-82 and heavier aircraft.

发动机试车,需经塔台管制许可。

ZYQQ AD 2.22 飞行程序 Flight procedures
1. 总则 General

除经齐齐哈尔塔台特殊许可外,在齐齐哈尔塔台管制区内的飞行,必须按照仪表飞行规则进行。

All flights within Qiqihar Tower Control Area shall operate under IFR unless special clearance has been obtained from Qiqihar Tower Control.

2. 起落航线 Traffic circuits

C、D类航空器:起落航线在跑道西侧进行,高300-500米; A、B类航空器:起落航线为左起落航线,高300米。

For aircraft CAT C/D: Traffic circuits shall be made to the west of RWY, height 300-500m; for aircraft CAT A/B: left-hand circuits, height 300m.

3. 仪表飞行程序 IFR flight procedures

严格按照航图中公布的进、离场程序飞行。如果需要,航空器可在空中交通管制部门指定的航路、导航台或定位点上空等待或做机动飞行。

Strict adherence is required to the relevant arrival/departure procedures published in the aeronautical charts. Aircraft may, if necessary, hold or maneuver on an airway, over a navigation facility or a fix designated by ATC.
