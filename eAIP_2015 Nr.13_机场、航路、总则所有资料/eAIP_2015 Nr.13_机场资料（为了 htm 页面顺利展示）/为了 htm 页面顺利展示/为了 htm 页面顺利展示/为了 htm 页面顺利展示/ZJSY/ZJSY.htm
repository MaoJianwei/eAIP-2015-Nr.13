ZJSY AD 2.20 本场飞行规定 Local traffic regulations
1. 机场使用规定 Airport operations regulations

1.1 禁止未安装二次雷达应答机的航空器起降;

1.1 Takeoff/landing of aircraft without SSR transponder are forbidden;

1.2 所有技术试飞需事先申请,并在得到空中交通管制部门批准后方可进行;

1.2 Each and every technical test flight shall be filed in advance and conducted only after clearance has been obtained from ATC;

1.3 可使用最大机型:B747同类及其以下机型。

1.3 Maximum aircraft to be available: B747 and equivalent.

2. 跑道和滑行道的使用 Use of runways and taxiways

2.1 禁止航空器在滑行道上做180°转弯;

2.1 180°turn around on TWY is forbidden for all aircraft;

2.2 D1、E1滑行道限制翼展超过52米的航空器滑行; E3、F2滑行道限制翼展超过65米的航空器滑行;

2.2 TWY D1, E1 are not available for aircraft with wing span more than 52m; TWY E3, F2 are not available for aircraft with wing span more than 65m;

2.3 滑行道B, C, F 为快速脱离道,使用26号跑道时通常使用滑行道F脱离跑道,使用08号跑道时通常使用滑行道C脱离跑道;

2.3 TWY B, C, F are rapid exit taxiways. In general, TWY C is used when RWY08 vacated, TWY F is used when RWY26 vacated.

2.4 跑道端掉头坪仅供翼展小于52米,主起落架外轮间距小于14米的航空器使用;

2.4 RWY08/26 turn pads are only available for aircraft with wingspan below 52m and outer main gear wheel span below 14m;

3. 机坪和机位的使用 Use of aprons and parking stands

3.1 所有进入机坪的航空器须由引导车引导;

3.1 Follow-me vehicle is available for aircraft entering apron;

3.2 发动机试车,须在夜航结束后经地面管制许可在并指定的地点进行,29-32 停机位为试车机位。试大车须在指定的位置进行,严禁在廊桥附近、客机坪和滑行道上试大车;

3.2 Engine run-ups are subject to Ground Control clearance, and shall be carried out at a designated location after the last night flight, stands Nr.29-32 are available for engine run-ups.

Fast engine run-ups must be carried out at designated location, and strictly forbidden in the vicinity of boarding bridges and on apron or TWYs;

3.3 停机位由132.00MHZ统一安排或调整;

3.3 Stands are managed by 132.00MHZ;

ZJSY AD 2.22 飞行程序 Flight procedures
1. 总则 General

除经塔台特殊许可外,在塔台管制区内的飞行,必须按照仪表飞行规则进行。

Flights within Tower Control Area shall operate under IFR unless special clearance has been obtained from Tower Control.

2. 起落航线 Traffic circuits

起落航线在跑道南侧,高度350-600米。

Traffic circuits shall be made to the south of runway, at the altitudes of 350m-600m.

3. 仪表飞行程序 IFR flight procedures

3.1 严格按照航图中公布的进、离场程序飞行。如果需要,航空器可在空中交通管制部门指定的航路、导航台或定位点上空等待或做机动飞行。

3.1 Strict adherence is required to the relevant arrival/departure procedures published in the aeronautical charts. Aircraft may, if necessary, hold or maneuver on an airway, over a navigation facility or a fix designated by ATC

3.2 仪表着陆系统敏感区保护程序

3.2 ILS Sensitive Area Protection Procedure

a. 当启用仪表着陆系统敏感区保护程序时,航空器须严格按照塔台管制员指令在B型等待位置等待。

a. When ILS Sensitive Area Protection Procedure is implemented, the aircraft shall follow the instructions of TWR Control to wait at RWY holding position Pattern B.

b. 当天气条件符合运行标准时,将不启用仪表着落系统敏感区保护程序。经塔台管制员许可后,起飞航空器可在跑道外等待位置等待,落地航空器进近方式可改为仪表着陆系统下滑台不工作。

b. When the meteorological conditions permit, ILS Sensitive Area Protection Procedure will not be implemented. With TWR Control clearance, take-off aircraft shall wait at RWY holding position, landing aircraft shall use GP INOP IAP. 

4. 雷达程序和/或ADS-B程序 Radar procedures and/or ADS-B procedures

三亚进近管制区域内实施雷达管制。航空器最小水平间隔为6千米。

Radar control within Sanya Appraoch Control Area has been implemented. The minimum horizontal radar separation is 6km.

6. 目视飞行程序 Procedures for VFR flights

6.1机场塔台管制区正式实施目视间隔和目视进近运行,此运行方式须得到ATC许可;

6.2从海口方向进场的航空器保持3000米过GIVIL后下降。

6.1 With the prior permission of ATC, visual separation and visual approach can be implemented within TWR control area;

6.2 The arrival aircraft from North shall keep 3000m over GIVIL, then descend.

9.1 Requirements for pilot:
