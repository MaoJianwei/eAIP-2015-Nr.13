ZUCK AD 2.20 本场飞行规定 Local traffic regulations
1. 机场使用规定 Airport operations regulations

1.1.禁止未安装二次雷达应答机的航空器起降;

1.1. Takeoff/landing of aircraft without SSR transponder is forbidden;

1.2. 所有技术试飞需事先申请,并在得到空中交通管制部门批准后方可进行;

1.2. Each and every technical test flight shall be filed in advance and conducted only after clearance has been obtained from ATC;

1.3.可使用最大机型:B747及其同类机型。

1.3. Maximum aircraft to be available: B747 and equivalent.

2. 跑道和滑行道的使用 Use of runways and taxiways

2.1 禁止航空器在滑行道上做180度转弯;

2.1 180°turnaround on TWY is strictly forbidden for all aircraft;

2.2 跑道运行规则:跑道运行模式为“隔离平行运行”,即:RWY02L/20R主要用于离港; RWY02R/20L主要用于进港。

2.2 General rules for the use of runways: Segregated parallel approaches/departures are implemented:.RWY02L/20R is mainly used for departure; RWY02R/20L is mainly used for arrival.

2.3 穿越跑道规则:穿越02L/20R跑道的滑行道为B1, B4, B5, B7, A6, A9, A11;

航空器应按照地面管制员指挥,滑行至跑道等待点外等待,然后向“塔台管制”提出穿越申请,收到塔台管制员穿越指令后,需尽快实施穿越,如有疑问,请在穿越前证实;

穿越跑道时,机组应注意监听塔台频率中其他有关跑道的指令或信息通报,并注意观察跑道及附近的活动。紧跟在起飞航空器后穿越跑道时,机组自行负责其与起飞航空器之间的距离以免受起飞航空器喷流的影响。机组应注意完整复诵管制员有关穿越跑道和跑道外等待的指令。

穿越结束后,机组需向塔台报告“已脱离跑道”;

2.3 Rules for crossing RWY: TWYs used for crossing RWY02L/20R are: B1, B4, B5, B7, A6, A9, A11; 

Following the instruction of GND Control, aircraft shall taxi to the holding position and hold short of RWY, then Request TWR Control for crossing clearance; conduct crossing upon approval; verify any questions prior to crossing; 

Pilot shall monitor the TWR FREQ for RWY information or instructions to other aircraft about RWY,observe the activities on the RWY and around;While crossing RWY after the taking-off aircraft, pilot shall be responsible for the safety distance with the aircraft to avoid the effect of wake turbulence.Repeat all the ATC instructions for clarity, then put in practice as soon as possible;

Finally, report to TWR Control‘RWY vacated';

2.4 禁止从B4, B5, B7, A9滑行道进入/脱离02L/20R跑道;

2.4 Aircraft are forbidden to enter/exit RWY02L/20R via TWY B4, B5, B7 and A9;

2.5 当RWY02L/20R用于进港时,除经管制员许可外,B1-B5、A11和E7之间区域不允许有航空器运行;

2.5 When RWY02L/20R is used for arrival, aircraft operation is strictly forbidden in areas between TWY B1& B5 and between A11& E7 without ATC permission;

2.6 为规范跑道占用时间,提高跑道容量,做出以下规定:

2.6 Requirement as follows to increase RWY operation capacity:

2.6.1 起飞航空器从等待位置到对正跑道应不超过60秒;

2.6.1 Departure aircraft shall finish RWY alignment within 60 seconds after leaving the holding positions;

2.6.2 落地航空器从接地到完全脱离跑道应不超过50秒; 

2.6.2 Landing aircraft shall fully vacate RWY within 50 seconds after touch down;

2.6.3 接到穿越跑道指令的航空器应在42秒内完成穿越;

2.6.3 Aircraft shall fully cross RWY within 42 seconds after getting ATC clearance;

2.6.4 航空器在运行中不能满足以上要求的,应提前通知管制单位。

2.6.4 If aircraft can not execute such operation requirement, flight crew shall inform ATC in advance.

2.7 当转换使用跑道方向过程中,使用跑道顺风分量大于3米/秒但不大于5米/秒时,管制员通知航空器驾驶员地面风向、风速后,指挥航空器短时顺风起飞或顺风着陆,如果航空器不执行该操作,航空器驾驶员应立即告知管制员并等待进一步指令。

2.7 During changing the direction of RWY in use, if downwind speed is more than 3m/s and not exceeding 5m/s, ATC shall inform ACFT the ground wind direction and speed, instruct downwind take-off or downwind landing for short time. If pilot decide not to take-off or land on downwind RWY, inform ATC immediately and wait for further instruction.

ZUCK AD 2.22 飞行程序 Flight procedures
1. 总则 General

除经重庆进近或塔台特殊许可外,在重庆进近管制区和塔台管制区内的飞行,必须按照仪表飞行规则进行。

Flights within Chongqing Approach Control Area and Tower Control Area shall operate under IFR unless special clearance has been obtained from Chongqing Approach Control or Tower Control.

2. 起落航线 Traffic circuits

起落航线通常在机场东侧,高度800-1200米。经空中交通管制部门许可,可在机场西侧进行。

Traffic circuits shall be normally made to the east of aerodrome, at the altitudes of 800m-1200m. Traffic circuits to the west of aerodrome are subject to ATC clearance.

3. 仪表飞行程序 IFR flight procedures

严格按照航图中公布的进、离场程序飞行。如果需要,航空器可在空中交通管制部门指定的航路、导航台或定位点上空等待或做机动飞行。

Strict adherence is required to the relevant arrival/departure procedures published in the aeronautical charts. Aircraft may, if necessary, hold or maneuver on an airway, over a navigation facility or a fix designated by ATC.

4. 雷达程序和/或ADS-B程序 Radar procedures and/or ADS-B procedures

4.1 重庆进近管制区域内实施雷达管制。在进近管制区域内,最小水平间隔为6千米,最小垂直间隔为300米。

4.1 Radar control within Chongqing APP has been implemented.The minimum horizontal radar separation is 6km, and the minimum vertical radar separation is 300m.

4.2 雷达引导与排序

4.2 Radar vectoring and sequencing 

4.2.1航空器在6000米以下,进入进近管制区域边界后,管制员对已识别的航空器提供雷达引导和排序,直至相应的最后进近航迹或目视跑道。根据航空器性能或管制室规定,发布雷达引导、上升或下降高度及速度调整指令,使航空器之间保持规定的雷达间隔或尾流间隔。

4.2.1 When entering Chongqing APP below 6000m, identified aircraft will be vectored and sequenced to the appropriate final approach track or to the time when RWY is in sight. Instructions about radar vectors, ascending/descending altitudes or speed adjustment will be issued so that stipulated radar intervals and wake turbulence intervals are maintained, taking into account aircraft characteristics or control regulations;

4.2.2 繁忙时段,雷达引导航迹将不同于公布的进、离场程序。航空器在得到雷达引导后,严格按管制员指令飞行;

4.2.2 During rush hour, radar vectoring track will be different with the track of STAR/SID published. Aircraft shall strictly follow the ATC instructions when obtaining radar vectoring service;

4.2.3 离场航空器在起飞前收到ATC放行或塔台管制员给出起飞限制,起飞后

将由管制员雷达引导加入标准或非标准离场航线。

4.2.3 Take-off limitation will be issued by delivery controller or TWR controller before take-off, and aircraft will be vectored to the standard or non-standard departure routes.

4.3 雷达管制规定

4.3 Radar control rules

4.3.1 有SSR应答机的航空器

a. 按照管制员要求开放A模式;

b. 开放应答机时应同时开放编码和高度,除非管制员另有要求。

4.3.1 For aircraft with SSR transponder

a. Set to model A as required;

b. Code and altitude should both set to open, except requird by ATC.

4.3.2 无SSR应答机的航空器,进入进近管制区时,应主动向管制员报告。

4.3.2 Aircraft without SSR transponder shall report to ATC controller before entering Chongqing APP.

4.3.3如机组已知应答机故障(包括无显示或显示错误),航空器在进入进近管制区域时应主动向管制员报告。

4.3.3 For aircraft with transponder mulfunction (including non-display or display error), pilot shall report to ATC controller before entering Chongqing APP.

5. 无线电通信失效程序 Radio communication failure procedures

5.1航空器在确定机载通信设备失效后,保持管制员给定的最后一个指令高度飞向长生桥导航台上空,按照标准等待程序飞行10分钟,然后使用02R跑道标准进近程序自主领航着陆;

5.1 When an airborne communication equipment failure is confirmed, keep the latest altitude assigned by ATC to Changshengqiao NDB ‘WX', hold 10 minutes following the standard holding procedure, then land to RWY02R according to the standard IAP.

5.2 已经进入起始进近的航空器继续按标准进近程序自主领航着陆。

5.2 Aircraft in the initial approach happen to communication failure, continue landing according to the standard IAP.
