ZYTX AD 2.20 本场飞行规定 Local traffic regulations
1. 机场使用规定 Airport operations regulations

1.1 禁止未安装二次雷达应答机的航空器起降;

1.1 Takeoff/landing of aircraft without SSR transponder are forbidden;

1.2 所有技术试飞需事先申请,并在得到空中交通管制部门和机场当局批准后方可进行。

1.2 Each and every technical test flight shall be filed in advance and conducted only after clearance has been obtained from ATC and the Aerodrome Authority.

2. 跑道和滑行道的使用 Use of runways and taxiways

2.1可以通过塔台申请引导车和拖车服务;

2.1 Follow-me vehicle service and towing service are available via Tower Control;

2.2 翼展52米及以上航空器从E滑行道上进入机坪后,只能使用机位Y01-Y04;翼展52米及以上的航空器从Y01-Y04机位滑出时,只能使用E进入跑道;

2.2 A/C with wing span more than 52m(inclusive)entering apron from TWY E shall park at stands Nr.Y01-Y04 only; A/C at stands Nr. Y01-Y04 with wing span more than 52m(inclusive) shall enter RWY by TWY E only;

2.3 禁止航空器在沥青道面上作180°调头;

2.3 180°turnaround on asphalt surface is forbidden for all aircraft;

2.4 In order to avoid frequency congestion, aircraft shall leave TWR frequency without radiotelephony instruction from controller ; aircraft shall keep the TWR frequency before departure or assigned by ATC clearance;

Departure aircraft shall pay attention to ATIS before start-up and apply for ATC clearance according to the frequency ATIS indicated. ATC shall clarify the contact frequency after leaving TWR frequency.

The delivery application shall be applied within 10 min before start-up. Flight crew shall execute the instruction within 5 min after getting the clearance, otherwise the flight crew shall apply for the instruction again;

2.5 离港航空器如需要长时间占用跑道(超过1分钟),必须在跑道等待位置向塔台管制通报占用跑道时间;进港航空器在着陆后应尽快(飞越跑道入口端至完全脱离跑道应在50秒内)脱离跑道,如需使用更长时间占用跑道或全跑道脱离应尽可能在着陆前通知管制员;

2.5 Departure aircraft shall inform ATC at the holding positions if occupy the RWY more than 1 min; Arrival aircraft shall vacate the runway as soon as possible (within 50 seconds from flying over RWY THR to vacating the RWY), otherwise inform TWR controller before landing;

ZYTX AD 2.22 飞行程序 Flight procedures
1. 总则 General

除经塔台特殊许可外,在塔台管制区内的飞行,必须按照仪表飞行规则进行。

Flights within Tower Control Area shall operate under IFR unless special clearance has been obtained from Tower Control.

2. 起落航线 Traffic circuits

起落航线在跑道南侧,A、B类航空器高度400米,C、D类航空器高度600米。

Traffic circuits shall be made to the south of RWY, at the altitude of 400m for aircraft CAT A/B, and 600m for aircraft CAT C/D.

3. 仪表飞行程序 IFR flight procedures

严格按照航图中公布的进、离场程序飞行。如果需要,航空器可在空中交通管制部门指定的航路、导航台或定位点上空等待或做机动飞行。

Strict adherence is required to the relevant arrival/departure procedures published in the aeronautical charts. Aircraft may, if necessary, hold or maneuver on an airway, over a navigation facility or a fix designated by ATC.

4. 雷达程序和/或ADS-B程序 Radar procedures and/or ADS-B procedures

进近管制区域内实施雷达管制,航空器最小水平间隔为6千米。

Radar control within Shenyang APP has been implemented. The minimum horizontal radar separation is 6km.

ZYTX AD 2.23 其他资料 Other information

全年有鸟类活动,每年九月、十月是鸟类迁徙季节,会出现成群家燕在飞行区集结的现象。机场当局采取了驱赶措施,以减少鸟群活动。

Activities of bird flocks take place all the year round, every september and october are birds migration season. There will be a phenomenon of barn swallows in the airfield. Aerodrome Authority resorts to dispersal methods to reduce bird activities.
